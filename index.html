Intro

Our website has had enough. We will not take it anymore and that’s what this is all about. To use a favorite term that all of you people really came up with, we will stop the weather. Today I will lay out just some of the evidence proving that we won this weather war award, and we won it by a landslide. This was not a close competition. I say sometimes jokingly, but there’s no joke about it, I’ve been in two competitions. I won them both and the second one, I won much bigger than the first.
